How To Save Your Small Business Through Covid.

Great tips for struggling business owners!

There is no doubt it has never been a more difficult time for restaurants, hair salons, breweries, and other small businesses. The question I have been asking myself is, how can we help small companies struggling to survive? I have done extensive research and have spoken with several business owners over the last 60 days. We have come up with three things that will help your small business in the unprevented time in history.

  • Try and connect with your customer base:

    • Now more than ever, you need to be proactive with your customer outreach. Ask for your customer's phone number, ask for Facebook likes, ask for Twitter follows, ask for home addresses. Use every avenue available to you to connect with your customer base. Send them personal thank you notes for their support. Go above and beyond to make that personal connection. We have found that a short personalized video message that shows your gratitude to the customer for coming in and supporting your business will have the most significant impact.

  • Be honest with your situation.

    • Don't be afraid to be vulnerable. If you are going through a tough time, it's ok to let people know that. Showing the human component of your business will help connect your customers to you, the business owner. These connections will be the driving force to keeping your business afloat during these difficult times.

  • Stay positive; you got this!

    • You are a proud business owner! You went against the grain when everyone told you couldn't. You find ways to get things done when others give up or walk away. There is no problem you can't overcome. The end of this turbulent period is on the horizon. Put all your energy into keeping your business going while remembering, good times are coming. You got this!
